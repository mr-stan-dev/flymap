# Mid Priority: Settings Summary Card

## Goal
Add a compact summary near the top of Settings that shows current theme and unit choices.

## Why
Users currently need to scan multiple rows to confirm their setup. A quick summary would make the screen feel more intentional and easier to read.

## Starting Points
- `lib/ui/screens/settings/settings_screen.dart`
- `lib/ui/screens/settings/viewmodel/settings_state.dart`

## Notes
- Keep it compact.
- This should summarize, not duplicate the full settings UI.
