# Offline Article Download Visibility

## Goal
Show more clearly which articles are already available offline.

## Why
Offline articles are a core product value, but the app should make offline availability more obvious in article lists and flight info views.

## Starting Points
- Learn list tiles
- flight info article views
- local article storage/progress state

## Notes
- A small badge or status line is likely enough.
- Do not turn the list into a file manager.
