# Settings Secondary Rows Density

## Goal
Make support and legal rows slightly denser and visually lighter than primary preference rows.

## Why
Those rows are secondary actions. They should not compete with editable preferences like Theme and Units.

## Starting Points
- `lib/ui/screens/settings/widgets/setting_item.dart`
- `lib/ui/screens/settings/widgets/leave_feedback_setting_item.dart`
- `lib/ui/screens/settings/widgets/rate_us_setting_item.dart`

## Notes
- Prefer small spacing and style adjustments over a full redesign.
- Keep tap targets comfortable.
